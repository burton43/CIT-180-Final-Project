Personal

Summary

I am currently employed by Tandem Ciders, where I work making hard cider. I am part of a three man team in charge of creating and packaging all the cider we produce. We work on sale goals and quotas so we are extremely self motivated and independent.

At work I monitor the ferments and check levels of acid, sugar and temperature to insure optimal flavor profile. We also press all the juice in house, so I run the press equipment. I drive forklifts, tractors and large diesel trucks; as well as diagnoses issue that the vehicles have from time to time. I run our canner, bottler, labeler, bottle washer, keg-washer, wine centrifuge, positive-displacement pumps, centrifugal pumps, and rotary lobe pumps; and do maintenance on all of them. I often have to rework our electrical plugs so I have experience wiring in 3 phase power.

I am going to school currently for software development. Computers have always been a passion of mine and I enjoy learning about and writing code. I hope to use my knowledge to maximize automation and organization.
